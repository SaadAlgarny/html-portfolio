The Best Movies According to Saad

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Shutter Island

This is my favourite film. I love its gripping psychological thriller narrative, captivating performances, and mind-bending twists.

Shawshank Redemption

Classic movie. I love its powerful storytelling and compelling characters.

Interstellar

Super beautiful film. It is a thought-provoking film that combines stunning visuals and an emotional storyline.
